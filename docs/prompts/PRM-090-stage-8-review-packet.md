Create a review packet for this artifact only:

[ARTIFACT NAME]

The review packet must be under 1,000 words.

Include:
- artifact being reviewed
- decision requested
- what changed
- what is in scope
- what is out of scope
- key risks
- acceptance tests
- open decisions
- evidence required
- recommendation: approve / revise / split

Do not include:
- full design text
- exhaustive rationale
- implementation ticket detail
- future phase detail
- appendices

The goal is to help a busy reviewer understand what decision they are making.

At the end include:
- Scope check
- Language/readability check
- Reviewer action requested
